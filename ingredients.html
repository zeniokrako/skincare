Glycerin

In 10 products

A powerful humectant (moisture-attracting agent) that can absorb its own weight in water. One of the most effective moisturizing ingredients, outperforming hyaluronic acid and urea. Helps improve skin barrier function, has antimicrobial properties protecting against bacteria and fungi. Non-comedogenic, suitable for all skin types including sensitive, oily, dry, and acne-prone. Most effective when combined with other moisturizing ingredients.

Niacinamide

In 5 products

A form of vitamin B3 (nicotinamide) with multiple evidence-based skincare benefits. Regulates sebum production, reduces inflammation, inhibits melanin transfer to treat hyperpigmentation, and strengthens skin barrier by stimulating ceramide production. Effective at 2-10% concentrations. Well-tolerated by most but can irritate some sensitive skin at higher concentrations. Can be combined with retinoids and vitamin C.

Panthenol

In 5 products

Provitamin B5 (converted to vitamin B5 in skin). Acts as both humectant and emollient - attracts water and seals it in. Promotes wound healing by stimulating epidermal regeneration and fibroblast proliferation. Anti-inflammatory properties help with UV redness and dermatitis. Strengthens skin barrier by stimulating lipid synthesis. Very gentle and safe for daily use. Effective at 0.5-5% concentrations.

Phenoxyethanol

In 8 products

A widely used preservative in cosmetics, considered one of the most well-tolerated preservatives available. Safe for all consumers at concentrations up to 1% according to European Scientific Committee on Consumer Safety. A rare sensitizer with one of the lowest risks of sensitization among preservatives. May occasionally irritate very sensitive or eczema-prone skin. Avoid use on infants under 3 months.
